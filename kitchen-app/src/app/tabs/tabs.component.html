Indian cuisine comprises of a wide variety of regional and traditional cuisines native to the Indian subcontinent.Given the range of diversity in soil type, climate, culture, ethnic groups, and occupations, these cuisines vary substantially from each other and use locally available spices, herbs, vegetables, and fruits. Indian food is also heavily influenced by religious, in particular Hindu, and cultural choices and traditions.[1] Also, Middle Eastern and Central Asian influences have occurred on North Indian cuisine from the years of Mughal rule.[2] Indian cuisine is still evolving, as a result of the nation's cultural interactions with other societies..
